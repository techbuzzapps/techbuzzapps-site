About Dharma Connect

Dharma Connect is a spiritual discovery platform designed to help devotees explore Hindu temples, festivals, deities, sacred destinations, devotional content, and cultural traditions.

Available for both Android and iOS.

Core Features

  • 🛕 Explore Hindu Temples
  • 🙏 Discover Deities
  • 🎉 Festival Information & Celebrations
  • 🕉️ Mantras & Spiritual Content
  • 📍 Pilgrimage & Religious Destinations
  • ⭐ Favorite Temples & Deities
  • 🏅 Badges & Gamification
  • 📖 Daily Spiritual Discovery
  • 📸 Temple Visit Experiences
  • 🗺️ Travel Wishlist
