Delete Your Dharma Connect Account

You may request deletion of your Dharma Connect account and associated data at any time.

How To Request Account Deletion

  • Email us at support@techbuzzapps.com
  • Use subject line: Account Deletion Request
  • Include your registered email address

What Gets Deleted

  • Your account profile
  • Saved preferences
  • Wishlist and visit data
  • Associated app activity data

Data Retention

Certain information may be temporarily retained where required for security, fraud prevention, legal compliance, or backup purposes.
