🗑️ Delete Your Dharma Connect Account

We respect your privacy and give you control over your data. You may request deletion of your Dharma Connect account at any time.

How to Request Account Deletion

  • 📝 Use the subject line: Account Deletion Request
  • 👤 Include your registered email address

  • ⭐ Wishlist and visit history

Deletion Timeline

We aim to process verified account deletion requests within 30 days.
